Our Process
1The Application

Interested participants will have to apply to be a part of the program. We are affording our beneficiaries an opportunity to discover themselves and develop soft skills needed to be competitive in whatever industry they choose to operate in.

2 The Interview

Successful applicants will undergo an interview with us. Beneficiaries including some irregular migrants who were returned home will also undergo this process to allow us get to know them. If we think they’d be a great fit for our program, we’ll move them to the next stage!

3 The Skill Acquisition & Mentorship Program

Once accepted, they’ll be in an enabling learning environment with fellow motivated individuals. During this period, the trainees will also be mentored through our clusters, cooperatives, partner networks and other online platforms owned by Genius Hub Global Initiative.

4 The Follow Up

At the end of the program, participants are awarded with a certificate. We will further provide a platform for them to engage, explore and network through our online marketplace where they advertise, generate leads and sell their various products and services.
