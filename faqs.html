What is Genius Hub?

Genius Hub is an organization (social enterprise) based in Edo state, Nigeria, founded in 20th August, 2015. Some of our services include MSME incubation, Employability skills acquisition (Digital, Business, Vocational and Technical skills), Access to Markets, Access to Grants, Shared workspaces, Training and consultancy, Technology, Media and movie distribution.
